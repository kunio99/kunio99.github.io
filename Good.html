Oishi Park

This place is on the north bank of Lake Kawaguchiko. In this area, photos can be captured with Lake Kawaguchiko and Mount Fuji.

Chureito Pagoda

It is a five-story pagoda on a hill overlooking Fujiyoshida City and Mount Fuji in the distance.

Oshino Hakkai

It is one of the highlight attractions of Yamanashi Prefecture. Of Japan Located near Mount Fuji. Most tourists who visit the Lake Kawaguchiko area.
